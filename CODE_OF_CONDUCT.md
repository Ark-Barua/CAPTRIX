# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

In the interest of an open and welcoming community, contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in CAPTRIX a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Use respectful and inclusive language.
- Be constructive in feedback and technical discussions.
- Assume good intent and focus on solutions.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or hateful language.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking fair, appropriate corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels related to CAPTRIX.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to project maintainers through GitHub repository contact channels.

All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban from community spaces.
